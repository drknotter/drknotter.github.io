Interests

I'm a mathematician by training, so I enjoy solving difficult problems. I also have a computer science background, so applying those skills towards solving difficult problems is something of my expertise. I try to do a little recreational coding every day.

I have many other interests outside academia, mathematics, and software. In my free time, I like to play Texas Hold 'Em poker, fold the occasional origami construction, watch movies, and go on Star Trek marathon binges. I play a lot of frisbee golf, and I enjoy listening to Bach, Mozart, and Beethoven. I'm also a big fan of Tower of Power, Bob Marley, Ray Charles, Miles Davis, Maynard Ferguson, and Chick Corea. My favorite food is a good ham and cheese sandwich.
